# Market Research Notes - Public

## AI Agent Market Overview

The AI agent market is experiencing rapid growth across multiple segments:

### Key Market Segments

1. **Enterprise AI Assistants**: $8.2B market, growing at 35% CAGR
2. **Developer Tools**: $3.5B market, growing at 28% CAGR
3. **Knowledge Management**: $4.1B market, growing at 22% CAGR

### Competitive Landscape

- **LobeHub**: Chief Agent Operator, strong ecosystem play
- **Ogra**: Local-first, transparent routing (differentiator)
- **Microsoft Copilot**: Enterprise distribution advantage
- **Anthropic Claude**: Strong safety positioning

### Notable Trends

1. Shift toward local-first and privacy-preserving AI
2. Growing demand for audit trails in regulated industries
3. Convergence of RAG and agentic workflows
4. Emergence of agent-to-agent (A2A) protocols
